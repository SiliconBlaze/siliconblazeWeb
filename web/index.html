About Us

siliconblaze is an initiative by university students interested in technology to impact the society by volunteering to teach computer programming to dis-advantaged youths
it also incorporates a dev shop and code shairing plartform for young developers, in a bid to nature young upcoming computer scientists
at siliconblaze we believe that you are never too young to make a difference

Empower Communities

we empower communities by giving youths the tools neccesary to fight the evils that burden today's communities i.e. hunger, poverty and disease

Peer to Peer

As iron sharpens iron, we do believe we youths can transform Africa's technological landscape by encouraging shairing of knowledge and ideas amongst ourselves

Future of Technology

While technology was written in the Silicon Valley, its future is being written in the savannahs of Africa, and we are at the fore-front by encouraging adoption of technology amongst Africa's youth

Quality

Africa deserves quality and we strive to deliver, we ensure our projects are thorough, educative and interactive inorder to nature the best developers

  • Mission

Africa is the silicon Savannah and we are the blades of grass that give Savannah its meaning. However, that is not all we need for the Savannah has also to be lit with knowledge. The Savannah has to burn with the desire to transform this continent. All it takes is for a lit blade of grass to light up the next blade and as sure that nothing spreads as first as bush fire, soon and very soon our Savannah will be up in flames of knowledge in IT and technology. And Bingo! What once was a laughing stock will be a stock of ideas and success. We are the lit blades of grass and we undertake to light the other blades. We take Computer Science to High Schools, we pledge to not only inspire but train, not only motivate but activate, not only pledge but indeed make a change. This we will accomplish through the computer science for High school project. Come and lets set the Savannah ablaze..

Transform Africa’s Technological landscape.
