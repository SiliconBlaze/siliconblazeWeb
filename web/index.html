About Us

siliconblaze is a community of like-minded young people who believe in the power of Africans for Africa.
We offer free IT training to youths in churches, schools, homes and even in the streets. Wherever they are.
We dedicate few hours of our time to tutor, mentor and guide fellow youths into computer programming in a bid to create and inspire solution solvers

Empower Communities

we empower communities by giving youths the tools neccesary to fight the evils that burden today's communities i.e. hunger, poverty and disease

Peer to Peer

As iron sharpens iron, we do believe we youths can transform Africa's technological landscape by encouraging shairing of knowledge and ideas amongst ourselves

Future of Technology

While technology was written in the Silicon Valley, its future is being written in the savannahs of Africa, and we are at the fore-front by encouraging adoption of technology amongst Africa's youth

Quality

Africa deserves quality and we strive to deliver, we ensure our projects are thorough, educative and interactive inorder to nature the best developers

Crime, poverty, Corruption, Hunger and inadequate access to education continue to frustrate Africa.
Good news, all these evils can be fought and won through adoption of Technology.
That's why we undertake to train youths on computer science basics in a bid to give them the tools to fight these evils

A initiative to realize Africa's dream of a silicon savannah by teaching and mentoring youths in computer programming

An Africa where technology is a tool we use to solve the problems (hunger, poverty and unemployment), that we face daily.
